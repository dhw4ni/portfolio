Project Name

An interactive 2D game designed specially for partially blind and dyslexic children of a government run school in Oman, Azzan Bin Qais International School. This project was completed over the period of 2 semesters. One for Research, Analysis and Documentation and the second semester was entirely dedicated to Design and Implementation of the application. I designed the tabs and templates on Adobe Illustrator and later imported them to Adobe Flash to build the application.
